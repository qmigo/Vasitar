Introduction

Vasitars Private Limited is a technology based company founded by alumni of IIT Bhubaneswar. The company is governed by faculty members of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) and Industry experts from diverse background in Pipeline technology. We are the first Indian Company to provide “Complete in-situ Composite Repair Solutions” to all kind of damage scenarios in transmission pipelines by using our Patent Registered innovation “Nano Filler Reinforced Polymer Composite Wrap to Repair Corroded Pipelines”. Our products are designed to provide structural reinforcement, leak repair, and corrosion prevention for pipelines and industrial pipe-works. Vasitars’ Solution rehabilitate strength of pipeline which is as good as the new pipe, allowing you to continue with your operations with same confidence as compared to replaced pipe with the new one.
