Our History

Vasitars Private Limited has been incorporated in India on 18th Day of January 2017 (CIN U74999DL2O17PTC3L0759) with a target to bring innovation in the field of transmission pipeline repair. The seed of this organization was sown in summers of 2015, when three students of IIT Bhubaneswar started their research on use of composite materials in repair of transmission pipeline. The hard work and academic excellence of these three students under the guidance of academic and industry

experts results into invention entitled "Nano Filler Reinforced Polymer Composite Wrap to Repair Corroded Pipelines". The product has been developed and tested on recommended standards listed under ISO, ASME and OSID. Satisfied with the lab test outcome, Vasitars Private Limited has launched the product at commercial levels.
